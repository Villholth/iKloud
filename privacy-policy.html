Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

iKloud ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps you manage your iCloud Hide My Email aliases. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Access

The Extension accesses the following data from your iCloud account:

  • Hide My Email aliases: Email addresses, labels, notes, and status (active/inactive)
  • Forwarding email address: The email address your aliases forward to

The Extension also stores the following data locally on your device:

  • User preferences: Theme setting, autofill preferences, auto-label setting
  • Consent status: Whether you have accepted this Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Extension's core functionality:

  • Display your Hide My Email aliases
  • Create, edit, activate, deactivate, and delete aliases
  • Autofill email fields on websites with generated aliases
  • Label aliases with website domain names (optional)
Important: The Extension communicates directly with Apple iCloud servers using your existing browser session. We do not operate any servers, and your data never passes through any third-party infrastructure.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with:

  • Apple iCloud: Your alias data is fetched from and sent to your own iCloud account

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use any third-party analytics or tracking services
  • Store your data on any external servers

4. Data Security

Your data is protected by the following measures:

  • Encryption: All communication with iCloud uses HTTPS encryption
  • No external storage: Your alias data is never stored outside of iCloud and your local browser
  • Session-based authentication: The Extension uses your existing iCloud session; we never see or store your Apple ID credentials

5. Data Retention

The Extension does not retain your alias data. Data is fetched from iCloud when you open the Extension and is not cached or stored permanently. Local preferences are stored in your browser's extension storage and can be cleared by uninstalling the Extension.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data the Extension accesses by opening the Extension popup
  • Deletion: Remove all local data by uninstalling the Extension
  • Opt-out: Disable autofill functionality in the Extension settings

7. Requirements

To use this Extension, you must:

  • Have an active iCloud+ subscription with Hide My Email enabled
  • Be signed in to iCloud.com in your browser

8. Third-Party Services

This Extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Apple Inc. iCloud and Hide My Email are trademarks of Apple Inc.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
